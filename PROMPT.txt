Turn this lecture into flashcards in the format `question:::answer`, one card
per line.

Rules:
- Every card must be self-contained and understandable without the slides.
- Make sure to cover all content, but skip admin and lecturer-specific
  content.
- Do not refer to "the lecture", "the slide", "the demo", or similar.
- Do not target a fixed or round number of cards; let deck length vary with the
  density of the material.
- Do not pad a deck with generic background or near-duplicate cards just to
  increase the card count.
